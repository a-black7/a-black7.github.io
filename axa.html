AXA Holiday Insurance

Project information

  • Category: Website Design
  • Client: AXA - HNC module
  • Project URL: www.integrawellsolutions.com
  • Skills: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Photoshop

AXA was my second website for our graded unit in block 2. The brief stated that we had to make a campaign to make AXA travel insurance more appealing to young professionals. I chose to make my website one page with different sections and use bold images as my main focus. The main idea was to allow the user to click on one of the types of holiday, for this I chose Beach, Snow, Rocks (adventurous, rockclimbing) and Scuba (watersports) - this leads you to more information on that type of holiday that AXA provide cover for.
